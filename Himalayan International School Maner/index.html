About Himalayan International School

Himalayan International School, Maner, is committed to holistic education for children from foundation years through senior secondary. We blend CBSE-aligned academics with co-curricular life, modern labs, and a caring environment rooted in our motto—Darkness to Light.

Academics

CBSE-oriented curriculum with emphasis on understanding, application, and board readiness at senior levels.

Play-based and activity-led learning; focus on language readiness, numeracy, motor skills, and social confidence in a nurturing environment.

Strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, EVS, and co-curricular exposure; regular assessment and parent engagement.

Subject depth across sciences, mathematics, languages & social studies; practicals in labs and project work.

Board-focused preparation for Classes 10 & 12; remedial support, library access, and career awareness.

Leadership

Messages from our Director and Principal — committed to quality education and the all-round development of every child.

Director’s message

Himalayan International School, Maner, has been established with a clear purpose—to offer children a space where academic rigour goes hand in hand with values, discipline, and care.

Over the years we have striven to strengthen our campus, faculty, and programmes so that every learner benefits from modern classrooms, laboratories, and a supportive community. Our motto, Darkness to Light, reminds us daily that education is about guiding young minds towards clarity, confidence, and responsibility.

I invite parents to partner with us in this journey and to visit the school to experience the spirit of Himalayan International School firsthand.

Principal’s message

As Principal, I am honoured to lead a team of dedicated teachers who work every day to make lessons meaningful—whether in the science lab, the library, the smart classroom, or on the assembly ground.

We focus on attendance, behaviour, and steady progress so that each child feels seen and supported. Parent–teacher partnership is vital; together we can reinforce habits of hard work, respect, and curiosity.

I look forward to welcoming you to our campus and to building a bright future for your child at Himalayan International School.
